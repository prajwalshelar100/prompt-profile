# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 2026**

Prompt Profile ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

### 1. Data Storage & Ownership
**On-Device Storage**: Prompt Profile is designed to be a "local-first" application. Your profiles, project data, product logs, and personal biometrics are stored locally on your device using a persistent database. We do NOT host your data on our servers.

### 2. Information We Collect
- **Camera Access**: Used solely to scan product labels and health records for data extraction. Images are processed on-device (via ML Kit) or temporarily via AI APIs (Gemini/OpenAI) if enabled.
- **Biometric Data**: You may choose to store Age, Height, Weight, and Blood Group. This data remains on your device.
- **API Keys**: If you provide Gemini or OpenAI API keys, they are stored securely in the device's Keychain/SecureStore.

### 3. Third-Party Services
If you enable AI features (Gemini or OpenAI), the app will send text or images to these services for analysis. This is subject to their respective privacy policies. We do not sell or share your data with other third parties.

### 4. Your Rights
Since data is stored locally, you have full control. Deleting the app or clearing its data will permanently remove all your stored information.

### 5. Contact
For questions regarding your privacy, contact us via the app's settings.
